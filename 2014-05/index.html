  • Two control functions: "listen" and "tell"

Traditional Observer Pattern

  • The Subject
    • Add Observer
    • Remove Observer
    • Notify Observers
  • The Observer
    • On Notify

First Class Functions, Awesome! Javascript Win!

  • The Subject
    • Doesn't care, or know, about Observers
    • Just tell the world what it has done and let the world sort it out.
    • There can be no observers! No one cares :(
  • The Observer
    • Does what ever it wants with action the Subject is telling the world.
    • Can do nothing.
    • Can do everything.
  • The Arbitrator
    • Cares about Subjects and Observers but doesn't care what is passed between.
    • Just a stack of functions to call when a key is requested.
    • Just a way to add a function to a key's stack.

Also could be the Event Queue Pattern.
